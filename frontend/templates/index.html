System Architecture

A complete end-to-end multi-modal diagnostic pipeline

🧠

Deep ANN Model

4-layer deep neural network with BatchNorm and Dropout to process 10 clinical tabular features with high interpretability.

👁️

ResNet50 Transfer Learning

Pretrained ResNet50 CNN fine-tuned on liver ultrasound images via two-phase transfer learning for robust feature extraction.

⚡

Multi-Modal Fusion

Concatenation of 32-dim ANN features and 64-dim CNN features, processed by 3 dense fusion layers for final prediction.

🔍

SHAP Explanations

SHAP values reveal which clinical features drove the ANN prediction, with force plots and bar charts for clinical clarity.

🌡️

Grad-CAM Visualization

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Maps highlight important regions in ultrasound images that informed the CNN decision.

📊

Patient Records & History

SQLite database stores patient info, lab values, images, predictions and XAI plots for longitudinal monitoring.

Diagnostic Classes

The system classifies liver condition into three categories

✅

Normal

All liver function tests within normal range. No structural abnormalities in ultrasound image.

ALT: 7–55 U/L AST: 10–40 U/L Albumin: 3.5–5.0 g/dL
⚠️

Fatty Liver

Elevated ALT/AST with increased echogenicity on ultrasound. Moderate risk requiring lifestyle changes.

ALT: 40–150 U/L AST: 35–120 U/L Bilirubin: 0.8–3.0 mg/dL
🚨

Cirrhosis

Severely elevated enzymes, low albumin, high bilirubin, and irregular liver surface on imaging.

ALP: >200 U/L Albumin: <3.0 g/dL Bilirubin: >2.5 mg/dL
